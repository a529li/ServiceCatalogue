Frequently Asked Questions
  • What is the service catalogue?
    The AI Service Catalogue is a central directory that lists all AI-related tools, platforms, and support services offered by the university. It helps you understand what each service does, how to access it, and who to contact for help.
  • How do I request a new service?
    You can request a new service by clicking “Feedback / Propose a Service” and filling out the form. There will be a space to describe the service you need, why you need it, and any relevant details. For urgent or time-sensitive requests, please reach out using the contact listed in the Additional Resources section.
  • Who maintains the service catalogue?
    The service catalogue is maintained by Information Systems & Technology (IST). They oversee updates, ensure the information stays accurate, and coordinate with service owners across campus to keep listings current.
  • How do I cancel or modify a service request?
    Submitted requests cannot be modified within the catalogue interface. If you need to cancel or update a request, please use the contact listed in the Additional Resources section.
